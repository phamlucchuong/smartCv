# BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (BRD) - SMARTCV PROJECT

## 1. Project Overview
*   **Project Name:** SmartCV - AI-powered CV Evaluation and Job Recommendation Platform.
*   **Document Purpose:** To clearly define the business requirements, practical context, and overall goals of the system to guide the development team.
*   **Scope:** Build a web-based platform serving 3 target groups: Candidates looking for jobs, Employers seeking talent, and System Administrators operating the platform. The model is similar to TopCV, VietnamWorks, and LinkedIn.

## 2. Business Context & Goals
*   **Core Problems (Pain points):**
    *   *For Candidates:* Writing a CV without knowing its suitability for a specific Job Description (JD). Often randomly sending applications without guidance on improving their skill sets.
    *   *For Employers:* Spending excessive time and cost manually reading each CV for initial screening. Lacking an integrated Application Tracking System (ATS) within the job portal.
*   **Proposed Solution (To-Be):**
    *   Develop a recruitment platform utilizing Artificial Intelligence (Llama 3 API via Spring AI) to automate CV screening, calculate matching percentages, and act as a mentor for job seekers.
*   **Initial Estimated Scale:**
    *   1,000 Registered Users.
    *   100 Job Postings.
    *   1,000 CV Uploads & 1,000 AI CV Evaluations.

## 3. User Personas
*   **Candidate:** Fresh graduates or professionals looking for a job. Need tools to automatically parse/upload CVs, check for errors, and receive modification suggestions from AI to increase their hiring chances.
*   **Employer / HR:** Representatives of company HR departments. Need to purchase job posting packages, receive AI-scored CVs, and manage candidates via an ATS Kanban board.
*   **System Admin:** The platform's operations team, managing transactions, approving company profiles, and viewing overall system analytics.

## 4. High-Level Business Requirements

### For Candidates
*   Securely register and authenticate accounts via Email/OTP.
*   Upload CVs (PDF/Word format) and use AI to analyze them.
*   Receive detailed analysis: Calculate matching score (%) with the desired JD, detect presentation errors, identify missing skills (Skill gap), and suggest an improvement roadmap.
*   Take assessment tests (IQ/EQ/Technical) before the formal interview.
*   Receive a list of suitable job recommendations based on the uploaded CV content.

### For Employers
*   Manage corporate profiles (Logo, description, website).
*   Post jobs and purchase service packages to push jobs to the Top or increase visibility. Payment transactions must be securely processed and stored.
*   Use the AI Auto-Screener: Automatically reject CVs (< 50% match), move to manual review (50 - 69%), or approve and forward to HR for excellent CVs (>= 70%).
*   Manage the recruitment pipeline (ATS Board): Drag and drop candidates through columns like Applied -> Interview Scheduled -> Interviewed -> Offer Sent -> Accepted/Rejected.
*   Receive 5 random mock interview questions generated by AI based on the candidate's specific CV.

### For System Admins
*   View global statistics Dashboard: Number of jobs, AI evaluation counts, revenue from Employer packages.
*   Manage resources, resolve disputes, and lock/delete accounts violating platform rules.

## 5. Financial Constraints & Feasibility
*   Primary revenue comes from packages sold to "Employers" (Job Posting Packages / Top CV viewing limits).
*   Financial transactions (Payments) must be guaranteed by Multi-document Transactions in MongoDB to ensure consistency.
